About Us

At Virutec, we're at the forefront of healthcare AI, specializing in next-gen diagnostics. Our mission is to diagnose diseases like COVID-19, TB, flu, and RSV from the sound of cough and voice. Leveraging advanced AI technology, we're pioneering a revolutionary approach to medical diagnostics.

We are proud to share that we are on a pathway to FDA approval for our innovative Class II medical device. Virutec started as a dedicated team from the MIT Sandbox, bringing together expertise and innovation to make a lasting impact on global healthcare.
