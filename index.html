About Us

At Virutec, we're pioneering the future of healthcare AI. Our platform is dedicated to creating a global standard for smartphone healthcare audio AI training data. We provide high-quality, standardized, and globally diverse datasets to catalyze the next wave of medical AI innovations.

Moreover, we're at the forefront of next-gen diagnostics. Our mission is to diagnose diseases like COVID-19, TB, flu, and RSV from the sound of cough and voice. Leveraging advanced AI technology, we're revolutionizing medical diagnostics and paving the way for a healthier world.

We are proud to share that Virutec has a clear pathway to FDA approval for our innovative Class II medical device. Our journey started as a dedicated team from the MIT Sandbox, bringing together expertise and innovation to make a lasting impact on global healthcare.

Our Vision

Virutec envisions a future where healthcare is not only reactive but also proactive. By harnessing the power of AI, we aim to provide early and accurate diagnoses, empowering individuals and healthcare professionals to take timely and informed actions. Our vision extends to creating a global standard for medical AI that is accessible and effective.

Technology Overview

Our technology is built on cutting-edge machine learning algorithms that analyze audio patterns to identify potential health issues. By focusing on the subtle nuances of coughs and voices, we can detect early signs of various diseases. The Virutec platform is designed to be user-friendly and accessible, making it a valuable tool for both individuals and healthcare providers.
